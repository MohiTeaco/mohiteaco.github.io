Frequently asked questions

How do online tutoring sessions work?

Sessions run over video call with a shared digital whiteboard, so we can work through problems together in real time — just like sitting side by side, minus the travel. All the student needs is a computer or tablet with internet.

Which VCE subjects do you tutor?

VCE Maths Methods Units 1–4, VCE General Maths Units 1–4, and VCE English Units 3–4. I also tutor Mathematics for Years 7–10, including students preparing to start VCE maths.

Do you help with SAC and exam preparation?

Yes — SAC and exam preparation is a core part of tutoring. That includes targeted revision, practice questions under time conditions, and going through past papers with detailed feedback.

How much does tutoring cost?

Rates depend on the subject and year level — get in touch below and I'll send through current pricing. The intro chat is always free, with no obligation to continue.

Can you tutor students outside Victoria?

Yes for Years 7–10 Maths, since the fundamentals are consistent across states. My VCE subjects follow the Victorian curriculum, so they're best suited to students at Victorian schools.

How often should my child have sessions?

Most students do one session per week, with extra sessions before SACs and exams. Consistency matters more than volume — a regular weekly session builds far more progress than cramming.
